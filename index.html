    • About MoneyTreeGangBangers

    MoneyTreeGangBangers is a dynamic hip-hop group from Soweto, Johannesburg, South Africa. They are widely known for their innovative sound and compelling lyrics. With a growing fanbase, they continue to push the boundaries of different genres.

    From street corners to studio nights, the group represents the spirit of young South Africans carving out their own lane in the music world. Their music dives into real experiences, ambition, struggle, and the energy of their community, all while experimenting with new styles and influences. MoneyTreeGangBangers is here to make noise, build culture, and create a legacy that speaks for where they come from and where they headed.
